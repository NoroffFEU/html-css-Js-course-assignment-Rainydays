About us

The Rainy days is a tale of passion, innovation, and a commitment to providing high-quality outerwear to customers. It all began in [1991],>

When our founder, [William Nikolai], identified a gap in the market for stylish and comfortable jackets that could withstand various weather conditions

Driven by a love for fashion and a desire to create functional yet fashionable outerwear , [William Nikolai] embarked on a journey to establish Jackets Store.

With a small team of dedicated individuals, they set out to design and manufacture jackets that would not only keep people warm and protected but also make a fashion statement.

Our founder's unwavering commitment to excellence led to collaborations with skilled artisans and manufacturers who shared the same vision.
